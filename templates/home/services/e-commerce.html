Types of E-Commerce Websites


There are different types of eCommerce websites that our eCommerce site developers can develop for you, and that's what people love about us very much.

B2B Ecommerce (Business-to-Business)

It's an online portal for electronic transactions between two or more companies of goods and services. For Example: You sell software to other businesses.

B2C Ecommerce (Business-to-Consumer)

A portal to connect companies directly to the consumer and share information and funds for goods and services. For Example: You can buy jeans pant from an online retailer.

C2C Ecommerce (Consumer-to-Consumer)

Here you are a consumer who will sell used or a new product to other consumers, likely through a third-party portal. For Example: You are going to sell your used phone on Bikroy or eBay or Olx to other consumers.

C2B Ecommerce (Consumer-to-Business)

Well, this one is a different posture to talk about. Here the transactions of services and goods occur between a consumer or any individuals to companies.
